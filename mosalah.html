Biography

Mohamed Salah is an Egyptian footballer who plays as a forward for the Egyptian national team
as well as the Premier League club ‘Liverpool.’ Born and brought up in Gharbia, Egypt, he transferred to the Swiss football club,
'FC Basel'. In January 2014, he transferred to 'Chelsea,' becoming the first ever Egyptian player to play with
Chelsea. Subsequently, he played for ‘Roma’ and ‘Fiorentina’ before signing a new contract with ‘Liverpool’ in 2017.
In 2017, he was named the 'African Footballer of the Year' by BBC and CAF.
